VIP Own Your Brilliance Master Class by USA's Tamiko Cuellar, Forbes Coach

Description


Ready for an electrifying PARTY IN GHANA? Join us at Africa's biggest DJ event this year live at the Accra International Conference Centre.

The Ghana DJ Awards Festival was created to celebrate and appreciate the talents of Disc Jockeys (DJs). The annual award show features performances by artists, and some of the awards of more popular interest are presented in a televised ceremony broadcast on Joy Prime, the leading entertainment channel in Ghana.

The Ghana DJ Awards also takes over the centre of the earth for an electrifying period of music and entertainment in Ghana. In addition to concerts, DJ Cypher shows and home of the Ghana DJ Awards Awards, the Ghana DJ Awards 3-day weekend will feature Nominees Jam, Pub Fest, seminars, various social events and celebrity appearances.
Ghana DJ Awards Festival will climax with the main awards broadcasting live from Accra International Conference Centre, September 7, 2019.
