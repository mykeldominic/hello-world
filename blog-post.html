The Chairman also talked about fund raising saying that the appeal letters will soon be out. BUDGET: The house worked on the proposed budget, and In the course of this; Mr. Dilinyeli Emmanuel suggested we divide the budget into several parts before taking it out to meet people.

Mr. Michael Oyilawu added that we shouldn’t just collect money from sponsors, but also make sure we advertise their brands like on red carpet banners and also when publicizing the event. Mr. Victor Ndule also encouraged members of the committee to not only contribute ideas but also money.
PROTOCOL: Mr. Charles made it clear to members that none of us should be seen sitting down in the course of the event. REPORTS OF SUB-COMMITTEES: The Chairman Engr.
